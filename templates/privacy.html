Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating spending summaries, and enabling account access. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is necessary to operate the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. While no system is completely secure, we are committed to keeping your information safe.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use your data for any purpose other than supporting the delivery of our service. We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. You may also request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
